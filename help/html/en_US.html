FBReader: an Introduction

Start reading.

Want to start reading right away? Click any cover below to download and open the book. To return to this text, select “Start Screen” in the menu.

Looking for more books?

Just take a look in the Network library. There are several catalogs containing a multitude of books, some for free, some for a small fee. You can even add your own OPDS catalogs to the list.

Any of the books already saved to your device can be opened from the Library.

Store your ebooks in the cloud.

Want to copy your ebooks from a desktop to another device? Or maybe you want to store your downloaded books in a safe space? FBReader Book Network is a cloud storage service that uses Google Drive™. Enable synchronization with the Book Network, and your book collection will be always available from any of your computers and mobile devices. Your books will be stored in your own space on Google Drive™. You can manage your collection from any browser.

Reading the same book on your mobile phone and tablet? Don't worry about looking up your page every time you switch devices. Enable FBReader synchronization on both or all devices, and the program will open your last reading position automatically.

Choosing ebook formats.

FBReader supports all popular text file formats. However, if you have a book in several formats, the best choice is ePub, mobi (a.k.a. azw3 or kindle), or fb2. Please keep in mind that FBReader is a free reader, and therefore does not open DRM-protected books. Non-text formats like PDF or DjVu are supported by FBReader plugins.

  • PDF plugin on Google Play
  • DjVu plugin on Google Play
  • ComicBook (CBZ, CBR) plugin on Google Play
  • All plugins on FBReader official site
